**Document Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-02

## 4. Target Users

### 4.1 Primary Users

**Pham vi:**
- **Do tuoi**: 12+ tuoi
- **Dia ly**: Cong dan Viet Nam (trong va ngoai nuoc)
- **Tech level**: Tu khong biet gi den advanced
- **Muc dich**: Nho ngay gio, chap, sinh nhat theo lich am

**Dac diem:**
- Co smartphone (iOS hoac Android)
- Quan tam den van hoa truyen thong
- Muon cong cu don gian, de dung
- Co the offline (vung sau vung xa)

### 4.2 Secondary Users

- **Nguoi gia**: Can giao dien don gian, chu to
- **Nguoi nuoc ngoai**: Song o VN, muon hieu van hoa
- **To chuc**: Chua, dinh, lang... quan ly nhieu su kien

## 5. User Personas

### Persona 1: Minh - Ban tre do thi (25 tuoi)

**Background:**
- Lam viec tai Ha Noi
- Ban ron, it ve que
- Muon nho ngay gio ong ba

**Goals:**
- Nhan thong bao truoc vai ngay de sap xep ve que
- Chia se voi anh chi em de cung nho
- Export sang Google Calendar de sync voi lich lam viec

**Pain Points:**
- Quen ngay gio vi ban
- Khong biet ngay am lich chuyen sang duong the nao
- Khong co tool phu hop

**Tech Savviness:** 4/5 (Advanced)

---

### Persona 2: Co Lan - Nguoi noi tro (45 tuoi)

**Background:**
- Song tai TPHCM
- Quan ly gia dinh
- Nho nhieu ngay gio cua ca ho

**Goals:**
- Co tool don gian de quan ly tat ca ngay gio
- Nhac nho de chuan bi cung som
- De su dung, khong phuc tap

**Pain Points:**
- Nhieu ngay gio, kho nho het
- Viet tay tren lich giay -> de quen
- Muon chia se cho con cai

**Tech Savviness:** 2/5 (Basic)

---

### Persona 3: Be An - Hoc sinh (14 tuoi)

**Background:**
- Hoc cap 2
- Muon nho sinh nhat ong ba, bo me (theo am lich)
- The he Z, quen voi smartphone

**Goals:**
- Giao dien dep, hien dai
- Widget tren man hinh chinh
- De them su kien nhanh

**Pain Points:**
- App lich thuong xau, kho dung
- Khong thich app phuc tap
- Muon personalize (mau sac, icon)

**Tech Savviness:** 4/5 (Advanced)

---

## User Needs Summary

| Persona | Primary Need | Key Feature | Tech Level |
|---------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| Minh | Remember gio while busy | Export to Google Calendar | Advanced |
| Co Lan | Manage many family events | Simple event management | Basic |
| Be An | Track lunar birthdays | Widget + nice UI | Advanced |
